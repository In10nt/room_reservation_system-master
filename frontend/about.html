About Ocean View Resort

Where Luxury Meets the Ocean

Our Story

Nestled along the pristine shores of Colombo, Ocean View Resort has been a beacon of luxury and hospitality since its establishment. Our journey began with a simple vision: to create a sanctuary where guests can escape the ordinary and immerse themselves in extraordinary experiences.

What started as a dream has blossomed into one of Sri Lanka's most celebrated beachfront destinations. Every corner of our resort tells a story of dedication, passion, and an unwavering commitment to excellence.

Today, Ocean View Resort stands as a testament to timeless elegance, offering our guests an unparalleled blend of modern luxury and traditional Sri Lankan hospitality.
